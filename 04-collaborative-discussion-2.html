Legal and Ethical views on ANN applications

According to Neumann el al (2022), there are two types of Artificial intelligence, the weak AI which is not capable of resolving issues independently, and self-ware or conscious AI also, know as AI singularity, that is capable to outperform human intelligence. Salvagno at el (2023) stated, for now the process summarising articles and compose scientific paper of using chat-bots and AI needs a human being specialised in the discipline revision to guarantee correctness, consistency, and authenticity. Similarly, it supports professors evaluating and rating student performance (Ahmad at el, 2022). Something that used to be a time-consuming tasks can be done automatically and in a fraction of the time it used to take.

On the other hand, as per Fyfe (2022), raised a extremely relevant question, whether a student uses AI assistance to compose his or her final paper should be considered cheating or not. According to Eke (2023), it is a breach the fundamental basis academic integrity if a student utilises AI to write an article or any other kind of academic paper. To avoid such issues, Hosseini at el (2023) publish an article that recommended the acknowledge and to list the use of AI in the writing process as reference, supported by the fact that not so long ago scientists made use of computer based programs to examine or transform images like Photoshop and, to explore and manipulate data, as example SPSS.
